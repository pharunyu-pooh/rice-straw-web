Why Choose Rice Straws?

100% Biodegradable

Our straws naturally decompose within 90 days, leaving no harmful residue in the environment.

All Natural

Made only from rice stems with no chemicals, additives, or preservatives added in the process.

Durable & Practical

Stays firm in hot and cold drinks for up to 4 hours without getting soggy or affecting taste.

Our Rice Straw Product

Each rice straw is carefully selected and processed to ensure the highest quality and safest drinking experience. Our straws are sourced directly from sustainable rice farms, supporting local agricultural communities.

Rice straws are completely tasteless, odorless, and don't break down in your drink, providing an excellent alternative to plastic, paper, or metal straws.
