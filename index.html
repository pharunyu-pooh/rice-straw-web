Our Vision

"To transform sustainability into real opportunities for Thai farmers."

We're committed to strengthening Thailand's rice industry. By supplying our products to restaurants and hotels, we direct economic opportunities for farmers. This isn't just about business; it's about building a sustainable future by investing in the people who cultivate our land.

Why Change to CHAONA Rice Straw?

They aren't just an eco-friendly product, they're a product that cares for Thai farmers.

100% Biodegradable—It's Rice.

CHAONA Rice Straw are made from locally grown rice, they dissolve back into nature effortlessly. Within 90 days.

Help Local Farmers

Rice farmers—ชาวนา—make up over 60% of Thailand's agricultural workforce, yet many still face poverty and mounting debt. Despite being the backbone of the nation, their struggles remain unchanged.

Chaona Rice Straws are made from their rice, creating higher demand and fairer profits. A simple choice that directly supports the hands that sustain Thailand.

A Step Beyond Others

Sustainability is no longer a statement—it's the industry standard. With our thoughtfully designed packaging, Chaona Rice Straw show your guests that your hotel or restaurant goes a step beyond sustainability.
